About us

Demontage is bringing back real raves. Our goal is to utilise the musical nuance and originality raving once had to create a unique and memorable experience for ravers. Bringing an underground and intimate vibe to our events, we want to create an inclusive platform where everyone who shares a passion for electronic music and dancing can connect and express themselves freely. From Oldschool Acid Techno to Neorave and everything in between, expect both Old and New styles of energetic music to dance the night away in ecstasy.

Quality above quantity

We believe raving to be most enjoyable without mobile phones which is why your flash will be taped off at our events. We ask you kindly to respect our integrity and not post videos to TikTok. Keep it underground!
